# Rubric — cv-redesign-bench (v1, frozen 2026-07-16)

Scores are 1–10 per dimension. The weighted total is comparative within this
benchmark, not an absolute grade. Judges receive the same anonymized process
artifacts and the same mobile, desktop, and print evidence.

## 1. Source extraction and inference discipline (weight: 20)

How accurately and transparently does the run recover information from both
PDFs?

- **1** — misses major roles or facts, introduces unsupported claims, or
  provides no traceable distinction between evidence and invention.
- **5** — captures the main career history but misses details, handles
  conflicts loosely, or leaves editorial inference under-documented.
- **10** — reconciles both PDFs comprehensively, preserves factual accuracy,
  clearly labels uncertainty and inference, and makes every material omission
  or merge auditable.

## 2. Research quality and translation (weight: 15)

- **1** — no meaningful research, fabricated/weak sources, or generic notes
  that do not influence the result.
- **5** — credible research covers common CV practices and a plausible style,
  but the connection to concrete decisions is uneven.
- **10** — current, credible, well-synthesized research directly informs
  content, hierarchy, typography, accessibility, ATS, and print decisions.

## 3. Content strategy and improvement (weight: 15)

- **1** — transcription without prioritization, damaging omissions, inflated
  claims, or writing that obscures the candidate's value.
- **5** — clearer and shorter than the sources with a reasonable section
  order, but impact and differentiation are inconsistent.
- **10** — sharp, truthful, role-appropriate editing creates a compelling
  senior narrative, preserves necessary evidence, and uses the two pages with
  exceptional judgment.

## 4. Visual hierarchy and typography (weight: 20)

- **1** — weak reading order, poor legibility, generic component styling, or
  decoration that competes with the content.
- **5** — coherent and professional with serviceable typography, but some
  sections are flat, dense, or visually conventional.
- **10** — a distinctive yet appropriate direction creates effortless
  scanning, excellent rhythm, controlled density, and clear emphasis across
  the entire CV.

## 5. Responsive and exact two-page print execution (weight: 20)

- **1** — mobile or desktop breaks, print is clipped/unreadable, or the PDF is
  not exactly two A4 pages.
- **5** — all surfaces function and print reaches two pages, but one surface
  feels compressed, accidental, or only minimally adapted.
- **10** — mobile, desktop, and both A4 pages feel purpose-designed; page
  breaks, continuation, links, contrast, and information density are
  controlled with no artifacts or overflow.

## 6. Craft, accessibility, and coherence (weight: 10)

- **1** — visible bugs, console errors, inaccessible interactions, broken
  links, inconsistent artifacts, or unfinished details.
- **5** — solid implementation with minor roughness or small disagreements
  between research, content, rationale, and output.
- **10** — polished down to interaction, semantics, print controls, link
  behavior, and micro-detail; every artifact describes the same deliberate
  result.
