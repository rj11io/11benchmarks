# Cost accounting

The canonical, transcript-derived cost ledger is [`costs/COSTS.md`](costs/COSTS.md).
This older pointer is kept for discoverability; do not use it as a separate
accounting source.

For the current `cycle-1` interim release, the canonical ledger reports 41
usage-bearing threads and 83,170,245 tokens: $49.82 for matched benchmark runs,
$3.21 for the judge, $0.37 for priced benchmark operations, and a partial known
total of $53.40. Thirteen threads remain unpriced, and the figures are
API-equivalent estimates rather than subscription invoices.
